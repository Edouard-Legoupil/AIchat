National Emergency Management Agency

The National Emergency Management Agency (NEMA) is a government agency which operates under the authority of Cabinet Office.

Our mission is to reduce the loss of life and property within the Commonwealth of The Bahamas, by ensuring that adequate preparedness and mitigation measures, and response and recovery mechanisms are established to counteract the impact of natural, man- made and technological hazards.
